Pasta and Sausage

Ingredient Amount
Orecchiete Pasta 1 lb.
Italian Sausage 4 links
Diced Tomatoes 1 can
Heavy Cream 1/4 cup
Parmesan Cheese 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 quarts of water
  2. Add the pasta
  3. In a separate pan, add the sausage (remove from casings)
  4. Once the sausage is browned, add the can of tomatoes.
  5. Drain the pasta when still al dente.
  6. Add the pasta to the sausage pan, along with the heavy cream
  7. Allow to reduce for a few minutes, add the parmesan before serving.
